Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package bulk breakfast sausag
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 (16 ounce) package hash brown potatoes
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 6 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 (2.64 ounce) package country gravy mix
  • 1 pinch ground paprika, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Combine pasta, water, marinara sauce, meatballs, peppers, and salt in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Cover tightly with aluminum foil and bake until pasta is al dente, 30 to 35 minutes.
  4. Remove the foil and stir in 1 cup mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle with remaining 1 cup of mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake, uncovered, until cheese is melted, 10 minutes more.
  6. Serve garnished with fresh herbs, if desired.

This savory sausage breakfast casserole with hash brown potatoes, green onions, and cheese is easy to put together and perfect for a hearty breakfast or weekend brunch. You can cook the sausage and green onions in a cast-iron skillet, and use that to bake the casserole instead of a baking dish.
